Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2025  ·  Last Updated: May 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Bar Rank ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Cooper Mattern, collects, uses, and protects information from users of the Bar Rank loyalty platform.

Information We Collect

We collect the following when a customer opts into Bar Rank at a participating venue:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Check-in activity and points history
  • Venue association (which bar or restaurant the customer is enrolled at)

How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used solely to operate the Bar Rank loyalty program, including:

  • Tracking loyalty points and leaderboard rankings
  • Sending SMS notifications about rank changes, challenges, and venue promotions
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving the platform

SMS Messaging

By opting in at a participating venue, you consent to receive SMS messages from Bar Rank. Messages may include loyalty updates, rank notifications, challenge alerts, and venue-specific promotions. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.

To opt out, reply STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or email barrank.llc@gmail.com.

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • The participating venue you enrolled at, for administering your loyalty rewards
  • SMS delivery providers (e.g., Twilio) solely to send messages you consented to receive

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting barrank.llc@gmail.com.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Children's Privacy

Bar Rank is intended for individuals of legal drinking age only. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a revised effective date.
