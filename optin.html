SMS Compliance & Opt-In Documentation

How Customers Opt In
to SMS Messages

This page documents Bar Rank's SMS opt-in process, consent language, message types, and opt-out mechanisms for carrier and regulatory review.

The Opt-In Process

How Customers Subscribe

Customers opt in to Bar Rank SMS messages by physically scanning a QR code displayed at a participating bar or restaurant. The opt-in is entirely voluntary and happens at the point of check-in.

No messages are sent before explicit consent is obtained. The SMS consent checkbox is unchecked by default and customers must actively check it before submitting.

1

Customer scans QR code at venue

QR code is physically displayed at the bar. Customer must be present at the venue to scan it.

2

Customer enters name and phone number

Fields are required to participate in the loyalty program.

3

Customer checks the consent checkbox

Checkbox is unchecked by default. Customer must actively agree to Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and SMS messages before submitting.

4

Consent recorded with timestamp

Opt-in timestamp and source are stored in the database at the moment of submission.

Actual Check-In Form
✓

Exact Consent Language Shown to Customers

The following text appears verbatim next to the consent checkbox on the check-in form. The checkbox is unchecked by default and must be actively checked by the customer before they can submit the form.

✓
I AGREE TO THE TERMS OF SERVICE AND PRIVACY POLICY. I CONSENT TO MY NAME APPEARING ON THE LEADERBOARD AND TO RECEIVING OCCASIONAL MESSAGES FROM THIS BAR VIA BAR RANK.

Customers who do not check this box cannot complete check-in. Consent is a prerequisite for participation in the loyalty program.

Message Types

What Customers Receive

🏆 Rank Change Notifications

Sent when a customer's leaderboard position changes at their enrolled venue.

"Bar Rank: You just got knocked to #2 at Tap House! Check in tonight to reclaim your spot. Reply STOP to opt out."

🎯 Challenge Alerts

Sent when the bar owner launches a new challenge or competition event.

"Bar Rank: New challenge at The Rail — First to 50 check-ins wins a $50 tab. You're at 12. Let's go. Reply STOP to opt out."

📣 Venue Promotions

Occasional promotional messages sent on behalf of the participating venue.

"Bar Rank: Happy Hour at Overtime Bar tonight 5-8pm. Show this text for a free first drink. Reply STOP to opt out."

🎖️ Loyalty Updates

Points confirmations, milestone achievements, and season updates.

"Bar Rank: You've hit 100 points at Tap House! You've unlocked the High Roller rank. Keep it up. Reply STOP to opt out."

Opt-Out & Help Keywords

STOP
Immediately unsubscribes the customer. Confirmation message sent. No further messages delivered. Opt-out is permanent until customer re-opts-in.
HELP
Returns support information including contact email barrank.llc@gmail.com and opt-out instructions.
START
Re-subscribes a previously opted-out customer if they choose to re-enroll.

Every message sent through Bar Rank ends with "Reply STOP to opt out." Opt-out requests are processed immediately and automatically.
