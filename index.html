The Process

Simple to Set Up.
Impossible to Ignore.

01

Bar configures Bar Rank

The venue sets up their leaderboard name, challenge goals, and rewards. Takes minutes — no technical knowledge required.

02

Customer scans the QR code

A QR code at the bar takes customers directly to the check-in page. They enter their name and phone number and opt in — that's it. No app, no password, no friction.

03

Points stack with every visit

Each check-in earns points. Challenges multiply them. The leaderboard updates in real time so everyone can see who's ahead.

04

SMS keeps the game alive

When a customer's rank changes, drops, or a new challenge drops — they get a text. That's what brings them back before the weekend.

05

Opt out anytime

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately. No questions asked, no friction.

📱

How SMS Messaging Works

Customers subscribe by scanning a QR code at a participating venue and voluntarily entering their phone number. Consent is explicitly obtained before any messages are sent.

Messages include: loyalty point updates, rank notifications, challenge alerts, and occasional promotional messages from the venue. Message frequency varies. Standard rates may apply.

Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for support, or email barrank.llc@gmail.com. Phone numbers are never sold or shared with third parties.
